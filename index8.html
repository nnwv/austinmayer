DIGITAL DESIGN & MARKETING

Bio: Austin Mayer (B. Arch, Cooper Union, 2018) is a Philadelphia based artist and designer realizing collaborative projects at the intersection of art, architecture and technology. Completed projects include Prolongations.org (2024, with Marcos Hering); Legiswipe Congressional Data Warehouse (2023, with Evan Piro); the Henry O. Tanner House Renovation Proposal (2022, with House Museum); Design with FRANK (2021, with Gabriel Munnich and Yaoyi Fan); When Under One Sky (2021, with Michelle Lopez); 4 Objects for the Fences of Grover Cleveland Playground Proposal (2019); Making: The 100 Newsracks for the Sidewalks of NYC (2018); The Divided Island of California: A Cross Border Surf Film (2018); participation in the Re-assembly of John Hejduk’s Jan Palach Memorial at Astor Place (2016); Drawing Jean Dupuy’s Floor Mirror for the Emily Harvey Foundation Archives (2015); and the RCA Squat Eviction Benefit Show (2012, Oakland, CA).
